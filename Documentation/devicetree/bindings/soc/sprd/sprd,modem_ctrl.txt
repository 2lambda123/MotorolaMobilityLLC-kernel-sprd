Spreadtrum mcd bindings

Required properties:
- compatible: must be "sprd,roc1-modem-ctrl".
- poweron-gpio: control modem power.
- reset-gpio: control modem reset.
- cpwatchdog-gpio: modem watchdog reset int.
- cpassert-gpio: modem assert int.
- cppanic-gpio: modem panic int.
Example:

	sprd-mcd {
		compatible = "sprd,roc1-modem-ctrl";
		poweron-gpio = <&ap_gpio 15 GPIO_ACTIVE_LOW>;
		reset-gpio = <&ap_gpio 16 GPIO_ACTIVE_LOW>;
		cpwatchdog-gpio = <&ap_gpio 29 GPIO_ACTIVE_LOW>;
		cpassert-gpio = <&ap_gpio 30 GPIO_ACTIVE_LOW>;
		cppanic-gpio = <&ap_gpio 31 GPIO_ACTIVE_LOW>;
	};
